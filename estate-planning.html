    Special Needs Trust

    For families with members who have disabilities, we offer specialized planning services. Special needs trusts require unique considerations that go beyond typical estate planning. Our collaboration with NetLaw and Hargrove Firm attorneys ensures that we can address the complex challenges of planning for individuals with special needs, promoting fairness, security, and equity for all involved parties.
